About Mastowork

Mastowork is a decentralized social media platform designed to give users control over their online presence. Built on the principles of open-source collaboration and privacy, Mastowork offers a friendly community space that values transparency and creativity.

Current Status

Mastowork is currently in active development and available to the public. While anyone can browse and explore, sign-ups are currently restricted to approved members only. This ensures a smooth, secure onboarding process as we continue to improve the platform.

Our Mission

We aim to empower users with a federated social experience, free from centralized control and censorship. Our mission is to support a diverse network of communities that foster meaningful connections and creativity.

Contact and Support

For inquiries or support, please reach out to our team at support@masto.work.gd. We welcome feedback and contributions to help shape Mastowork's future.

While we continue to build and expand, you can also connect with other instances on the federated network to enjoy a wider social experience.
