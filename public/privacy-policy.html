Mastowork Privacy Policy

Welcome to Mastowork. As a Mastodon server, we operate as part of the broader Mastodon federated network.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Mastowork, we collect and process the following information:

  • Account details such as username, email address, and profile information.
  • Content you post and interactions within the Mastodon network.
  • Technical data including IP addresses, device information, and browser type.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain your Mastowork account and access to the Mastodon network.
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse or violations of our policies.
  • Improve the service and communicate important updates.

3. Federation and Data Sharing

Mastowork participates in the Mastodon federated network. This means:

  • Your public posts and profile information are shared with other Mastodon instances you interact with.
  • Other Mastodon servers may cache or display your public content according to their own policies.

We do not control other instancesâ€™ policies and recommend reviewing their privacy terms if you interact outside Mastowork.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide services. You can request account deletion at any time.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. Please use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication where available.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access and update your profile information.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Control who can see your posts and profile through privacy settings.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at support@masto.work.gd.
